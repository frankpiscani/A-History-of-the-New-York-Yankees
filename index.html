New York Yankees

A Brief History

One of professional baseball’s oldest teams, the New York Yankees have become synomous with success.

The interlocked N and Y is perhaps the most recognizable sports logo in the world and stands for something greater than baseball. It’s a pop culture icon.

Over the course of more than a century, the Yankees have been the home of baseball legends such as Babe Ruth, Mickey Mantle, Yogi Berra, Derek Jeter and Mariano Rivera.
